Our chain link fence installation services provide affordable, durable fencing solutions for residential and commercial properties throughout Bucks County.

## Chain Link Fence Benefits

- **Most Affordable** - Lowest cost fencing option
- **Highly Durable** - Galvanized steel lasts decades
- **Low Maintenance** - Occasional cleaning only
- **Versatile** - Residential, commercial, and industrial uses

## Chain Link Fence Options

### Coatings
- **Galvanized** - Standard silver finish, rust-resistant
- **Vinyl Coated** - Black, green, or brown color options
- **Aluminum Coated** - Enhanced corrosion resistance

### Heights
- **Residential** - 4', 5', or 6' heights
- **Commercial** - 6', 7', or 8' heights
- **Industrial** - Up to 12' available

### Privacy Options
- **Privacy Slats** - Inserted for visual screening
- **Windscreen** - Fabric privacy screening
- **Barbed Wire** - Security enhancement (commercial)

## Common Applications

- **Backyard Enclosures** - Pet and child containment
- **Commercial Properties** - Security perimeter fencing
- **Sports Fields** - Baseball backstops, tennis courts
- **Construction Sites** - Temporary security fencing
- **Industrial Facilities** - High-security installations

All chain link fences include professional installation with concrete footings and tension wire for stability.
